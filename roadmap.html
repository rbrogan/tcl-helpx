Roadmap

We generally take the approach that the next release will go out on-time, and prefer to cut functionality rather than change the date.

The information below is subject to change.

0.2
2015.05.04

For the next step prior to a completed v1.0, we will add in the data access API. With this you can populate your own database and bundle it to make for CLI-based help.

1.0
2015.07.06

We will finish v1.0 by adding support for generating HTML pages and .chm files. This will require the release of Tetrama (our macro processor / template engine) to use.


Want to receive notifications about changes to Helpx? You can subscribe to the announcements mailing list by sending an email to helpx-announce-subscribe@robertbrogan.com. Put subscribe for the subject. (No need for anything in subject or message body.) You will get an email every time we have a new release.

Want to see what other features that may be coming? Check out the wishlist.

Do you have a special request for something? You can use the feedback page and let us know. It never hurts to ask!
